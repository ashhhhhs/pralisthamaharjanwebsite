About Pralistha Maharjan

I am a Kathmandu based artist dedicated to developing a visual world of mythic faces, character studies, cultural symbols, and hand-made texture.

Every artwork starts as a feeling in the making, then becomes a face, a mark, or a small story built through color, graphite, and patience.

With 20+

artworks across acrylic paintings, graphite studies, character concepts, and personal commissions.
